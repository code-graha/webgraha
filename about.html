About WebGraha

A small studio building considered digital experiences — websites, apps, and brand systems for businesses that want to feel as deliberate online as they are in person.

Our Story

Built around craft, not headcount

WebGraha started with a simple frustration: most small and mid-sized businesses are stuck choosing between cheap, templated websites and slow, expensive agencies. We set out to be the alternative — a focused studio that moves fast, charges fairly, and treats every project with the same precision regardless of size.

Every engagement is handled directly, end-to-end — from the first conversation to the final deploy — so nothing gets lost in translation between client, designer, and developer.

Common Questions

FAQ

What does WebGraha do?

WebGraha designs and builds websites, apps, and brand identities for small and mid-sized businesses, handling every project directly from first conversation to launch.

How long does a typical website project take?

Most web design projects take four to six weeks from kickoff to launch depending on scope; app projects and full rebrands typically take longer.

Does WebGraha work with clients remotely?

Yes. WebGraha is a remote-first studio and works with clients globally over video calls, email, and shared project boards.

What is included in a WebGraha engagement?

Depending on the project, engagements can include discovery and strategy, UI/UX design, development, content support, and post-launch revisions.

How do I start a project with WebGraha?

Reach out via the Start a Project form on the WebGraha homepage, WhatsApp, or email — most enquiries get a response within one business day.
