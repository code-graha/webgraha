Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything we're most often asked before a project starts. Still have a question? Get in touch.

What does WebGraha do?

WebGraha designs and builds websites, apps, and brand identities for small and mid-sized businesses, handling every project directly from first conversation to launch.

How long does a typical website project take?

Most web design projects take four to six weeks from kickoff to launch depending on scope; app projects and full rebrands typically take longer.

Does WebGraha work with clients remotely?

Yes. WebGraha is a remote-first studio and works with clients globally over video calls, email, and shared project boards.

What is included in a WebGraha engagement?

Depending on the project, engagements can include discovery and strategy, UI/UX design, development, content support, and post-launch revisions. See the Services page for a per-service breakdown.

How do I start a project with WebGraha?

Reach out via the contact page, WhatsApp, or email — most enquiries get a response within one business day.

How much does a website cost?

It depends on the number of pages, how custom the design is, and what functionality you need beyond an informational site. We scope every project after a discovery conversation rather than off a price list — see the blog post on website pricing for a full breakdown of what drives cost.

Do you offer support after the site launches?

Yes. Post-launch revisions are part of a WebGraha engagement, and we stay involved after handoff rather than disappearing once the invoice is paid.

Can I see examples of your work?

Yes — real, live client projects are listed on the Portfolio page, not just mockups.

What's the difference between branding and web design?

Branding is the identity system itself — logo, color, type, voice, and guidelines for how they're used. Web design is where that identity gets applied to a specific product, your website. Most clients need both, but they're separate engagements with separate timelines — see how long branding takes for a realistic timeline.
